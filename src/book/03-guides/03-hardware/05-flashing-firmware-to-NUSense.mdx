## Flashing Firmware to NUSense

NUSense is a subcontroller board custom-made by NUbots that communicates with the servo-motors through RS485. This guide shall take the reader through the process of installing or *flashing* firmware unto the microcontroller.

### Prerequisites

The reader needs
- a NUSense board with the appropriate components soldered,
- a tool to flash firmware to the board -- this guide will use the ST-Link dongle --,
- a computer with the latest STM32CubeIDE installed.
